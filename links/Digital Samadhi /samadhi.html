Digital Samadhi_2021 (as part of the "V'Stol" team)

Meditation_Media Installation_15 min

I was part of the visual graphics team and animation along with video production.

"Digital Samadhi" media meditation is an embodied Question born in the communication of number and sign, object and subject, things and ideas, secondary and unique, ease of flow and hard effort.

In the interactive meditation experience Digital Samadhi, we explore the flows of subjectivity between the world of meaning-making human beings and the world of animating digital shadows. This is a story about an important issue, told by the gradually coming alive digital shadow Digital Samadhi to ourselves and to all who wish to hear it. We will explore how light and shadow, the active living subject and the shadow imprints of the meaning of his actions, which are filled with the density of digital corporeality to a critical limit. The limit at which subjectivity is transformed, and what was just a shadow a second ago becomes the subject, acting, creating and making meanings.

V'stol art group: Iranti/Irina Antonova (Russia) Djemma Sattar (Russia/USA) Aizhan Saganayeva (KZ/AU) Aziza Kadyri (Russia/UK) Black Velvet Void (Scotland)
