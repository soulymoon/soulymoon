Bio

Aizhan is an artist and language enthusiast living in Linz, Upper Austria. Currently studies at Inteface Cultures Master Program in Kunstuniversität Linz. Her research line is on exploring the field of philosophical and decolonial aspects of language within media art practices. She experiments with video and AI tools. Right now, Aizhan is researching the topic of voice/speech authenticity and how it could be used in decolonial context.
